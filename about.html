About PSD Raju

Christian songwriter, composer and worshipper, sharing songs that glorify Christ and encourage believers.

For many years, PSD Raju has composed Christian songs with one purpose—to glorify God and proclaim the hope found in Jesus Christ. His songs are rooted in Scripture and born out of personal faith, prayer and worship.

This website has been created to preserve these songs and make them freely available to believers around the world. It is our prayer that every lyric will encourage hearts, strengthen faith and draw people closer to Christ.

Whether sung in churches, homes or personal devotion, may these songs continue to point every listener to the love, grace and faithfulness of our Lord Jesus Christ.
