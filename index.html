Welcome

Welcome to the official collection of Christian songs composed by PSD Raju. These songs were written to glorify Christ, encourage believers, and share the hope found in Jesus Christ.

These songs were composed over many years as an offering of praise to God. They are shared here freely with the prayer that they will encourage believers, strengthen faith, and point hearts to Jesus Christ.
